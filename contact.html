Our Story

SL Hire is a leading concrete finishing company based in Australia. We utilise state-of-the-art laser-guided screed technology to produce superior results in concrete finishes. Our owner and operator, Stephen Wollschlager, has over 15 years of Laser Screed experience and has been assisting concretors in North Queensland, Central Queensland, and Northern Territory with placing and finishing concrete for over 25 years.
