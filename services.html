Services

Formwork

Formwork Solutions

We can achieve any formwork project, ranging from, sewage treatment plants to multi storey high rise developments and more.

Quality Materials

We are diverse with all formwork systems, such as Ischebeck Formwork System for decks, Doka and Peri for wall forms and lift cores.

Skilled Workforce

Our team has the experience and knowledge to execute any formwork project, regardless of its complexity or size.

Concreting

Place and Finish of Concrete

We specialise in flat floors to FF and FL numbers. Placing concrete and finishing in challenging conditions all year round, from wharfs to large tanks, to Bunnings floors.

Experienced Professionals

We have over 25 years' experience with placing and finishing concrete in North Queensland, Central Queensland, and the Northern Territory.

Laser Screed Technology

We use Somero laser screeds to screed our concrete to help achieve our FF and FL numbers. We utilise a 3D system which is fitted to our laser screeds, supporting the placement of concrete for outside areas such as hard stands and car parks.

Ride-On Trowel Machines

We have a fleet of ride-on trowelling machines for breaking the slab, panning machines and finishing machines fitted with plastic blades if required.

Laser Screed and Ride-ons Hire

S-240 Laser Screed

The S-240 Laser Screed is a reliable machine that delivers high-quality flooring. It's well-suited for outdoor work such as car parks, hard stands, and warehouse floors.

SXP Laser Screed

The SXP Laser Screed is an upgraded model that's excellent for the same types of work as well as high-end flooring projects like Bunnings warehouses and Coles/Woolworths supermarkets, which require super flat floors.

Ride-On Trowel Machines

We have 8 ride-on trowel machines. All machines can be fitted with pans, multi-combination blades, and finishing blades. Additionally, we have plastic finishes for hire.

Truss Screeds

We have two Allen truss screeds that can be adjusted from 4m to 20m for wide concrete pours. These machines can be used for high-slump concrete.
