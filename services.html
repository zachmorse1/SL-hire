Laser Screed Hire

S-240 Laser Screed

S-240 Laser Screed is a highly advanced concrete leveling machine that uses laser technology to ensure precision and accuracy in large-scale concrete flooring projects.

SXP Laser Screed

SXP Laser Screed is a newer model that offers some additional features and improvements over the S-240, such as increased maneuverability and a larger screed head for faster and more efficient concrete leveling.

Precision and accuracy

Our state-of-the-art S-240 and SXP Laser Screeds use laser guidance technology to ensure precise and accurate concrete placement and leveling.

Superior finish

Ensures a precise, level surface with minimal imperfections and reduces the amount of time and labor required for the project.
