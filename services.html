Our Equipment

S-240 Laser Screed

The S-240 Laser Screed is a state-of-the-art piece of equipment that uses laser guidance technology to place and level concrete with precision and accuracy. This results in a super flat finish that is perfect for warehouse slabs, hard stands, and larger scale car park pavements.

SXP Laser Screed

The SXP Laser Screed is a more compact version of the S-240, making it perfect for smaller job sites. Like the S-240, it uses laser guidance technology to provide a super flat finish to the concrete surface.

Laser Guided Plough

The Laser Guided Plough is a specialized piece of equipment that is used to prepare the subgrade before the concrete is placed. It uses laser guidance technology to ensure the subgrade is level and flat, providing a solid foundation for the concrete.

Ride-On Trowel Machines

Our ride on trowel machines are used to provide a smooth and polished surface to the concrete finish. They are perfect for achieving a high-quality finish on large job sites and can be hired with or without an operator.
