Laser Screed Hire

We offer laser screed hire services to provide precise and accurate concrete placement and levelling, resulting in a super flat finish that is perfect for a range of applications.

Let us know about your up-and-coming project so we can can assist you in producing a superior concrete finish.

Our technology has revolutionized the way concrete is placed and leveled today. By utilizing laser screeds, we can assist in reducing labor costs and increasing volume production, while producing super flat floors. Our services are appropriate for all warehouse slabs both inside and outside, hard stands, and larger scale car park pavements.

At SL Hire, we offer a range of services including screeding concrete using laser screeds and 3D profilers, as well as training and working with crews in placing concrete in any weather conditions throughout Queensland. We provide aluminium ramps for overpost tension slabs and ride on trowel machines, which can be hired with or without operators.

Our team is committed to delivering a high-quality finish every time. Prior to commencing work, we sit down with your team to negotiate the best approach for your pour and regularly undertake toolbox and prestart meetings. We also provide PowerPoint training for all procedures related to working with special concrete and working in and around laser screeds.

Our Equipment

All of our equipment is regularly maintained and serviced to ensure maximum efficiency and accuracy on every job.

S-240 Laser Screed

The S-240 Laser Screed is a state-of-the-art piece of equipment that uses laser guidance technology to place and level concrete with precision and accuracy. This results in a super flat finish that is perfect for warehouse slabs, hard stands, and larger scale car park pavements.

SXP Laser Screed

The SXP Laser Screed is a more compact version of the S-240, making it perfect for smaller job sites. Like the S-240, it uses laser guidance technology to provide a super flat finish to the concrete surface.

Laser Guided Plough

The Laser Guided Plough is a specialized piece of equipment that is used to prepare the subgrade before the concrete is placed. It uses laser guidance technology to ensure the subgrade is level and flat, providing a solid foundation for the concrete.

Ride-On Trowel Machines

Our ride on trowel machines are used to provide a smooth and polished surface to the concrete finish. They are perfect for achieving a high-quality finish on large job sites and can be hired with or without an operator.
